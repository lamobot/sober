# Sober

A clean and motivating iOS app to track your sobriety journey.

## Overview

Sober helps you stay motivated on your sobriety path by tracking the days you've been sober, calculating money and time saved, monitoring health improvements, logging your mood, and celebrating achievements.

## Features

- **Sobriety Counter**: Track days, weeks, months, and years of sobriety
- **Savings Calculator**: See how much money and time you've saved
- **Health Milestones**: Monitor your body's recovery with science-backed health improvements
- **Mood Tracking**: Log your emotional state with expandable mood cards and notes
- **Achievements**: Unlock badges for reaching sobriety milestones
- **Smart Notifications**: Customizable reminders (daily, weekly, or monthly)
- **Bilingual Support**: Full support for English and Russian with auto-detection

## Technologies

- **SwiftUI** - Modern declarative UI framework
- **Combine** - Reactive state management
- **UserDefaults** - Local data persistence
- **UserNotifications** - Push notification system
- **MVVM Architecture** - Clean separation of concerns

## Requirements

- iOS 16.0+
- Xcode 14.0+
- Swift 5.7+
